In order to move through the story you must visit the indicated points in Porto city. There are 8 places to travel to, 8 chapters of the story and 8 audio files for you to listen to.
Points will be awarded if you complete certain challenges.

1. Listening to the audio file (mandatory on the first chapter) - 20 points

2. Read the first part of the story - 10 points

3. Arrive to the destination on time / not on time - 40 points/25 points

4. Read the second part of the story - 10 points

5. Share a photo on Facebook - 20 points
